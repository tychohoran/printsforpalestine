Prints For Palestine

An Art Auction for Healthcare Workers in Gaza

About the Auction
October 22 - 27
Donate Directly

Prints for Palestine is an art auction organized to raise funds for Medical Aid for Palestinians, an organization directly supporting Palestinian healthcare workers on the ground. All of the works featured in the auction were generously donated by more than 50 artists. Prints for Palestine will launch at 12pm on Sunday October 22nd and wil close at 12pm on Friday October 27th.
